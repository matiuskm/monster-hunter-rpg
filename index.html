How to Play Monster Hunter RPG

Getting Started

Welcome to Monster Hunter RPG! You are a brave adventurer setting out to hunt monsters and become stronger.

Basic Actions

  • Hunt Monsters: Enter the cave to find monsters to fight
  • Visit Shop: Buy potions and upgrade your equipment
  • Use Potions: Heal yourself during or outside of combat

Combat

  • Attack: Deal damage to the monster
  • Run: Try to escape from combat (not always successful)
  • Use Potions: Heal yourself during combat

Potions

  • Small Potion: Heals 20 HP (10 gold)
  • Medium Potion: Heals 40 HP (30 gold)
  • Large Potion: Heals 60 HP (100 gold)

Tips

  • Keep an eye on your health and use potions when needed
  • Buy better weapons to deal more damage
  • Fight monsters near your level for best results
  • Run away if you're low on health
